Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check-in to a stall?

Use the "Scan for Check-in" button on your dashboard when you are near the stall's QR code. Alternatively, use the manual check-in form if authorized.

What if I forget to check-out?

Your session will automatically expire after your shift ends, but for accurate attendance, please ensure you scan out before leaving.

How can I change my assigned zone?

Zone changes require administrative approval. Please visit the main office or raise a request through Account Management.

Reporting equipment issues

In your dashboard, navigate to "Maintenance & Issues" and use the "Report Issue" button to notify the maintenance team.

Updating business category

Go to Profile View > Edit Profile to update your business details. Some changes may require document re-verification.

Staff size limitations

Each stall has a maximum staff capacity based on zone safety regulations. Check your stall details for specific limits.

Still need help?

Our support team is available 24/7 to assist you with any issues.
